Pasta recipe

Description

Learn how to make pasta at home! This 4-ingredient homemade pasta recipe is easy to make, and it yields chewy, delicious noodles every time.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make a nest with the four on a clean work surface
  2. Use your hands to gently mix in the flour
  3. Knead
  4. shape the dough into a ball and wrap it in plastic
  5. slice it into 4 pieces
  6. flatten it into an oval disk
  7. fold the dough
  8. cut and cook the pasta
  9. enjoy the pasta
