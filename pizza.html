Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Make perfect pizza at home with this classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos.

Ingredients

  • warm water
  • dry yeast
  • bread flour
  • virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • sugar
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • chicken or meat

Directions

  1. make and knead dough
  2. let the dough rise
  3. preheat the pizza stove
  4. divide the dough in 2 balls
  5. prep the toppings
  6. flatten the dough in a circle shape
  7. brush the dough with olive oil
  8. spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle toppings
  9. slide pizza in oven
  10. bake
  11. enjoy
